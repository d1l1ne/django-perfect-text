PerfectText

Help


This web-application corrects your text by deleting multi spaces and unnecessary spaces before and after punctuation marks, quotation marks, brackets, dashes; fixing sentence separation by adding spaces after '.' and capitalizing first letter if it's necessary. Please choose urls/emails option correctly, make an application know if your text contains any of them. Example: user's mistake in this option can change "google.com" to "google. Com". You can always use "My text contains urls or email adresses" but process may take a little bit more time.

Example:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet , consectetur adipiscing elit .pellentesque vehicula.

Processed text:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Pellentesque vehicula.
